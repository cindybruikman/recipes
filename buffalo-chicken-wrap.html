Buffalo Chicken Wrap

Home

Description

These buffalo chicken wraps are a delicious and protein-packed meal, featuring tender marinated chicken, fresh vegetables, and a flavorful honey mustard dressing. Perfect for a quick lunch or meal prep!

Ingredients

  • 450 g (1 lb) Chicken Breast
  • 3 g (1 tsp) Garlic Powder
  • 1.9 g (½ tsp) Paprika Powder
  • 95 ml (⅓ cup) Frank's RedHot Wings Buffalo Sauce & Greek Yogurt
  • Black Pepper, to Taste
  • 7.5 ml (½ Tbsp) Olive Oil
  • High-Protein Wraps
  • Honey Mustard Dressing
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • Sliced Cucumber
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken
  2. Cook the Chicken
  3. Rest & Slice
  4. Assemble the Wraps
  5. Wrap & Serve
